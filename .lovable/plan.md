

## Digital Penta — Premium Futuristic Website Build Plan

### Design Direction
- **Dark-first, ultra-premium** aesthetic with muted tones — Deep Space Black (#0A0F1E) base, subtle violet (#6C63FF) and cyan (#00D4FF) accents used sparingly
- Typography-driven design: "Plus Jakarta Sans" for headings, "Inter" for body, "JetBrains Mono" for tech accents
- Glassmorphism cards with frosted blur, gradient glow borders on hover
- Minimal but intentional CSS animations (fade-ins on scroll, smooth hover states, subtle parallax)
- Bento grid layouts for feature sections, generous whitespace

### Phase 1 — Core Pages (This Sprint)

**1. Homepage (Award-Winning Hero)**
- Full-viewport dark hero with animated gradient mesh background, kinetic headline "Five Powers. Infinite Possibilities."
- Stats bar with animated counters (500+ Projects, 12+ Countries, ₹100Cr+ Revenue, 98% Retention)
- 5 service pillars showcase with glassmorphism cards in horizontal scroll
- "Why Digital Penta" section with 3-column layout + animated SVG
- Case studies slider with metric overlays
- Industry expertise infinite scroll strip
- Testimonials masonry grid with trust badges (Clutch, Google Partner)
- Blog preview (3 cards) + Footer CTA with gradient background

**2. Five Service Category Pages** (Digital Marketing, PR, Development, AI, Automation)
- Reusable template: Hero banner → Problem/Solution → Process steps → Sub-services grid → Tools/tech logos → Pricing 3-tier cards → FAQ accordion → CTA section
- Each with unique accent color matching the brand pillars (violet, cyan, green, gold, orange)

**3. About Page**
- Founder story section with text reveal
- Mission/Vision bold typography
- Team grid with hover bio reveals
- Company timeline (horizontal milestones)
- Awards wall + office locations with maps

**4. Contact Page**
- Split layout: premium multi-field form (left) + contact info/map (right)
- Country code selector, service multi-select, budget dropdown
- WhatsApp button for Middle East users

**5. Get Proposal Page**
- Multi-step wizard form (5 steps) with visual progress indicator
- Card selection for services, goal checkboxes, budget slider
- Animated success confirmation

### Phase 2 — Sub-Service Pages (35+ pages)

**Templated sub-service pages** for all services:
- 8 Digital Marketing sub-pages (SEO, PPC, Social Media, Content, Email, Influencer, Video, Performance)
- 7 PR sub-pages (Media Relations, Brand Reputation, Crisis, Digital PR, Press Release, Thought Leadership, Event PR)
- 7 Development sub-pages (Website, Mobile App, E-commerce, Web App, CMS, API, UI/UX)
- 7 AI sub-pages (Strategy, Chatbot, Content Gen, Predictive, Computer Vision, NLP, AI Marketing)
- 7 Automation sub-pages (Marketing, Workflow, CRM, Sales, Social, Reporting, WhatsApp)

Each follows the service template with unique SEO-optimized content, keywords, and meta tags.

### Phase 3 — Supporting Pages

- **Portfolio/Work page** with filterable project grid
- **7 Industry pages** (Real Estate, Healthcare, Education, E-commerce, Finance, Hospitality, SaaS)
- **5 Location pages** (India/Delhi, Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Riyadh, Doha)
- **Blog listing + article template**
- **Privacy Policy & Terms of Service**
- **Custom 404 page** with search + popular links

### SEO Architecture (Built Into Every Page)
- Semantic HTML5 structure with proper heading hierarchy
- JSON-LD Schema markup (Organization, Service, FAQ, Breadcrumb, LocalBusiness)
- OpenGraph + Twitter Card meta tags on all pages
- SEO-optimized title tags following formula: `[Service] Agency in [City] | Digital Penta`
- Breadcrumb navigation on all inner pages
- Internal linking hub-and-spoke model
- Target keywords from your master plan embedded in page content

### Shared Components
- Transparent → frosted glass sticky navbar with mega menu for services
- Full-screen mobile nav overlay with staggered animations
- Sticky "Get A Proposal" CTA button
- Reusable sections: FAQ accordion, pricing cards, testimonial grid, stats counter, CTA banner
- Footer with 4-column layout, newsletter signup, social links, office addresses

### Responsive Design
- Mobile-first: 320px → 768px → 1024px → 1440px → 1920px
- Touch-optimized on mobile, WhatsApp redirect for Middle East mobile users
